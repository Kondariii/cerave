Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy CeraVe products?

CeraVe products are available at major drugstores, retailers, and online marketplaces. Use our store locator to find a retailer near you.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase with original receipt. Please contact the retailer where you purchased the product for specific return policies.

Are your products tested on animals?

CeraVe does not test any of our products on animals. We are committed to ending animal testing across our industry.

How can I find the right products for my skin?

Visit our Skincare Guide section or contact our customer service team for personalized recommendations based on your skin type and concerns.
