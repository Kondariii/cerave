About CeraVe

Developed with dermatologists, backed by science

Our Story

Founded in 2005, CeraVe was developed with dermatologists to create a line of skincare products with three essential ceramides and patented MVE Technology. Our mission is to provide therapeutic skincare for all.

Today, CeraVe is one of the fastest growing skincare brands in the United States, offering a complete line of products that cleanse, treat, and moisturize the skin.

Our Technology

MVE Technology

Our patented MultiVesicular Emulsion (MVE) technology releases moisturizing ingredients slowly over time, allowing them to penetrate deep into the skin to hydrate where it's needed most.

  • 24-hour hydration
  • Controlled release
  • Deep penetration
